# Getting started

Welcome to voces.ch. To launch your first campaign or petition, you first need to create an account and register your organization.

## 1. Create your account and verify your email

Before you can start, you need a personal user account:

1. Go to the [voces.ch registration page](https://app.voces.ch/register) and enter your details.
2. After submitting, you will receive an email from us.
3. Click the **confirmation link** in that email to verify your address and activate your account.

> **Note:** If you do not receive an email, please check your spam folder.

## 2. Register your organization

Every campaign on voces.ch belongs to an organization (or initiative/collective). The first time you sign in, you will be asked to create your organization.

Fill in these three fields:

- **Organization name:** The official name of your group (for example _Collective Concrete and Bass_). This name is used internally and for management.
- **URL slug:** A web-friendly short version of your organization name. It must contain no spaces or special characters and is lowercase only. It is usually generated automatically, but you can edit it.
  - _Example:_ If your organization is called "Climate Strike Zurich!", a good slug is `climate-strike-zurich`.
- **Default language:** Select your primary language. It will be preselected for new campaigns (you can still add more languages later per campaign).

## Next steps

Done. After registering your account and organization, you can immediately create your first campaign.
