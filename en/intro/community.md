# Community and support

Do you have a question, found a bug, or have a great idea for a new feature? Join our official voces.ch community.

[Go to the voces.ch community](https://community.voces.ch)

To provide better support and transparent product development, we run a dedicated forum for campaign creators.

## What you will find in the forum

- **Announcements:** Stay up to date on new releases, integrations, and features.
- **Ideas and feature requests:** Missing something? Share your idea and vote on others.
- **Bug reports:** If something is not working as expected, let us know.
- **Questions and support:** Exchange with other users and get help setting up your campaign.

> **Language note:** Switzerland is multilingual, but our team does not speak all four national languages. Please write forum posts in English when possible so everyone can follow along and we can support you faster.
