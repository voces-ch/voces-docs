# What are integrations?

With voces.ch, you collect signatures. But what happens to the data afterward? To connect supporters to your existing workflows, voces.ch provides **integrations**.

An integration links your campaign to external tools automatically. As soon as someone signs your petition, voces.ch sends the data in real time to your newsletter platform (for example Mailchimp) or automation tools (such as Zapier, Make, or n8n).

## How integrations work

- **In the background:** Integrations run asynchronously from the signature flow. Supporters see success feedback immediately without waiting for third-party services.
- **Organization-specific:** Running a campaign with partners? Each organization configures integrations for itself only. Your data goes to your systems; partner data goes to theirs.
- **Pause anytime:** Use the **Active** switch (`is_active`) to pause and resume integrations without deleting settings or credentials.
